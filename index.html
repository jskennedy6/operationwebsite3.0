Thanks for visiting this cool page about Teslas. Tesla was founded in 2003. They make electric vehicles and renewable energy products. Watch the video below to see a Tesla doing something awesome!



Here are some fun facts about Teslas:

  1. Tesla Cybertruck
    • The Cybertruck's design was inspired by movies and features an ultra-hard stainless-steel exoskeleton
    • The Cybertruck has a towing capacity of 11,000 pounds and can accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in 2.6 seconds. The top-of-the-line "Cyberbeast" model has three electric motors and 834 horsepower.
    • Armor glass: Can resist the impact of a baseball at 70 mph or class 4 hail

  2. Tesla Model Y
    • The Model Y has won several awards, including the 2024 Auto Trader New Car of the Year
    • The Model Y is a compact SUV that's slightly larger and has more cargo space than the Model 3
    • The Model Y can seat five people in its standard configuration, or seven with the optional third row

  3. Tesla Model X
    • The Model X has a range of up to 335 miles, which is greater than its competitors from Mercedes, BMW, and Audi.
    • The Model X has unique rear doors that open like gull-wing doors. They have multiple hinges and are designed to avoid hitting other cars or walls.
    • The base Model X has two electric motors, one in the front and one in the rear, which provide all-wheel drive.
